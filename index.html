METRPOLITAN TRANSIT AUTHORITY OF NEW YORK

New York City's public transportation system services millions of people a day but lacks the advancements and technology that have made the rest of the city so great. Our redesign focuses on the way users access public transportation through the Metro Card, payment machines, and turnstiles.

MEDSCAPE FROM WEBMD

Medscape develops and hosts desktop and mobile platforms for medical professionals to access clinical resources, latest treatment options, and earn medical education credits. By conducting usability tests for new ad interfaces, we were able to provide a seemless user experience.

METAMORPHIC PETROLOGY DATABASE

The MetPetDB redesign focused on refining user experience of two core functionalities, the live filtered search and image map for sample comparisons, by conducting user research, testing, and analysis.

RENSSELAER BACKTESTER

Backtester crowdsources digital backwork curation by providing students with an electronic storage space to submit work if they have taken the class and retrieve backwork if they are currently taking the class.

CONFLUENCE

The Confluence branding project emphasized our mission to bring together individuals of all ages in Troy, NY, to a common meeting point for the purpose of continuing education and intellectual stimulation.
