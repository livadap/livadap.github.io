METROPOLITAN TRANSIT AUTHORITY OF NEW YORK

New York City's public transportation system services millions of people a day but lacks the technology and efficiency advancements that have made the rest of the city so great.

The redesign of the system focuses on the way users access public transportation from a monetary standpoint through more secure and responsive Metro Cards, payment machines, and turnstiles.

MEDSCAPE FROM WEBMD

Medscape develops and hosts desktop and mobile platforms for medical professionals to access clinical resources, latest treatment options, and earn medical education credits.

As a User Experience design intern, I conducted usability tests for new ad interfaces to offer readers in-article content suggestions and successive acting-taking without taking away from the overall educational experience.

METAMORPHIC PETROLOGY DATABASE

The MetPetDB national database services academia with an extensive filtered search and image-map visualization tool with chemical comparison functionality.

As the User Experience researcher and User Interface designer, I maintained a focus on refining the user experience to better focus on the core functionality of the database. Through user interviews, testing, and analysis, the redesign was able to maintain a clearer focus.

RENSSELAER BACKTESTER

Students heavily rely on older copies of homeworks, tests, and quizzes called backwork. However, the overall inflexibility of having a physical product prevents multiple students from benefiting from its value.

Backtester crowdsources digital backwork curation by providing students with an electronic storage space to submit work if they have taken the class and retrieve backwork if they are currently in need of the material.

CONFLUENCE

Confluence is a city community center located in Troy, NY with a heavy emphasis on inspiring curiosity in individuals of all ages through the offering of study spaces for individual learning and group collaboration.

The Confluence branding project emphasized our mission to bring together individuals of all backgrounds, students and working-class with the hopes of stimulating intellectual growth.
